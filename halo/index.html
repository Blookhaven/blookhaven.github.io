Halo of the Bun

The Halo of the Bun is an expertly crafted parody of the Halo of the Sun - a symbol which first appears in Silent Hill 3. This design incorporates similar geometry to its inspiration. It hilariously references the universally venerated "It's bread." meme, about which nobody has ever once complained, or called "low effort", and likely never will.

The sigil incorporates various symbols and glyphs, including Pagan Celtic symbols, alchemical symbols, zodiac signs, Rovásírás (old Hungarian runes), Egyptian hieroglyphs and Inuktitut syllabics (an abugida-type writing system used in parts of Canada), as well as original symbols and pictograms.

Basic Ingredients

The outermost symbols represent the basic ingredients of bread; Flour, yeast, water and salt.

Lammas
The symbol for Lammas, a Pagan Celtic festival marking the wheat harvest. Used here to represent flour.
Fermentation
The zodiac sign for Capricorn. When used in alchemy, this symbol represents the process of fermentation. Here, it is used to represent yeast, which ferments, causing the bread to rise.
Water
The alchemical symbol for water.
Salt
The alchemical symbol for salt.

Large Rings


The outer ring represents hunger; A state that can occur in the absence of bread.

The inner ring represents satiation; A state that can be achieved via the consumption of bread.

Surrounding Symbols

The symbols that appear between the two large rings.

Farmer
This symbol depicts farming tools; A sickle and a hoe, along with the symbol for Lammas. It represents wheat farming for the production of bread.
Miller
This symbol depicts a mill, used to make flour for the production of bread.
Baker
This symbol depicts baking tools; A rolling pin, a whisk, and a paddle. For making bread.
